Drexel One AI Chatbot

Inclusive AI-powered UX research for international students

UX Research Lead Â· Academic Research Project

Problem

International students struggled with navigation, language clarity, and accessibility in DrexelOne, leading to task failure and frustration.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Led UX research strategy
  • Designed multilingual and accessibility-focused studies
  • Synthesized qualitative and behavioral data
  • Collaborated with faculty and students

What I Did

  • Identified key barriers through interviews, surveys, and task testing
  • Designed AI-assisted support concepts to reduce cognitive load
  • Prioritized cross-cultural clarity over direct translation

Impact

+80% user satisfaction
+89% task success rate
Improved comprehension for Farsi-, Spanish-, and Chinese-speaking users

Key Takeaway

This project shows how AI can be used to enhance inclusivity without replacing human judgment.
