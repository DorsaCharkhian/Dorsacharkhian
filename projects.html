Drexel One AI Chatbot

UX Research Lead

International students were struggling with a confusing university portal. I ran interviews and usability tests to understand why - turns out, direct translation was making things worse, not better. We shifted to clearer explanations and AI-assisted help. Task success went from frustrating to 89%.

Interviews, usability testing, surveys • 30 students

Supporting Parents in Childcare

UX Researcher & Service Designer

Parents told us they didn't trust the random health advice they found online - they wanted to talk to a real person. So instead of building another app with more content, we designed services that connected them directly to nurses and pediatric professionals.

Interviews, journey mapping, service design • Parents

Fashion Technology VR Initiative

UX Researcher & Designer
